Finding Mark, the Bois, and their Density

Climate change is a big problem. This is because Copepods are migrating further north (among other things). To figure out exactly what happens, we created a model of the density of Copepods over time. This model wasn't 100% accurate, but we did find Mark and also the Bois.

Mark to Mars

Yeah sending humans to space is cool, but have you ever sent a Copepod to space? We didn't think so.

The great thing about Copepods is they can withstand a much higher acceleration (g force) than humans. So sending one to mars was much easier. Mark leaped into the rocket and we executed a carefully modelled 3 stage mission to mars. First, the rocket was launched into a geostationary transfer orbit. Then, we planned a Hoffman Orbital Transfer, launching Mark into a clean circular orbit around Mars. The final descent didn't go quite as well, but Mark ended up on mars.

One small saunter for Copepod, one giant leap for Copepodkind

Cloudberry Jam

Cloudberry Jam is a tool for musicians to play together online. It is currently in development and is designed around a 'latency chain' concept to allow musicians to play without worrying about latency. We are designing this system as a webapp that is simple to use, but still has the necessary features to make it useful.
